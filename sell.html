MISSION

Agree.exchange is a platform to help people reach agreements easier.

WHY

Negotiating is a skill and not everybody have it. While solving a conflict of interest may result in some relief, wouldn't be better if we avoid the conflict entirely?

HOW

1) There are two roles in the platform, SELLER and BUYER.

2) The SELLER submits a FINAL OFFER including the MINIMUM AMOUNT that could accept and his email to generate a LINK.

3) The SELLER shares that LINK and the BUYER then enters the MAXIMUM AMOUNT willing to pay.

4) A FINAL outcome is immediately provided that SATISFIES both parties, sending the SELLER an email notification and invalidating the link.
